Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the Service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal third-party infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers) to operate the Service. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only as instructed by us.

We do not integrate with any advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
